Secret Santa Generator

This is a simple and private Secret Santa generator that helps you generate random pairings for your Secret Santa gift exchange.

Features:

  • Free and Open Source: The website is completely free to use, and the source code is available on GitHub under the AGPL 3.0 license.
  • Privacy First: We prioritize your privacy. That means no cookies, no email, and participant names are never sent to any server.
  • Customizable Options: Tailor your Secret Santa exchange to your group's preferences. You can freely add or remove participants, and even set exclusions to avoid certain pairings.
  • Convenient Sharing: Easily share the generated pairings with your group using a group link or individual links for each participant. Note that anyone with access to the group link, has access to all the pairings; fairness is at the discretion of the group participants.
